# PSP Partnership Program

## Overview

The PSP Partnership Program is designed to create a thriving ecosystem around the Persistent Sessions Protocol by forming strategic alliances with key players in the browser automation, testing, and development tools space.

## Partnership Tiers

### 1. Technology Partners

**Criteria:**
- Integrate PSP into their core product
- Minimum 10,000+ users
- Technical compatibility requirements
- Joint go-to-market commitment

**Benefits:**
- Co-marketing opportunities
- Priority technical support
- Joint case studies
- Conference speaking opportunities
- Revenue sharing agreements

**Current Targets:**
- Playwright team (Microsoft)
- Selenium project
- Cypress.io
- Puppeteer team (Google)

### 2. Integration Partners

**Criteria:**
- Build connectors/plugins for PSP
- Maintain integration quality
- Provide customer support for integration

**Benefits:**
- Listed in official integrations directory
- Technical documentation support
- Joint webinars and content
- Early access to new features

**Current Targets:**
- CI/CD platforms (GitHub Actions, GitLab CI)
- Testing frameworks (Jest, Mocha, PyTest)
- Cloud providers (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure)

### 3. Solution Partners

**Criteria:**
- Provide implementation and consulting services
- PSP certified consultants on team
- Case studies and success stories

**Benefits:**
- Listed in partner directory
- Lead referrals
- Partner certification program
- Marketing support

**Current Targets:**
- Testing consultancies
- DevOps service providers
- System integrators
