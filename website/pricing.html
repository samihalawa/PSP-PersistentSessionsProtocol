Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any differences in your next billing cycle.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all your sessions and data at any time. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted for security.

Do you offer custom enterprise pricing?

Absolutely! Enterprise customers get volume discounts, custom terms, and dedicated support. Contact our sales team for a personalized quote based on your needs.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You can explore all features and decide what works best for your team.
